What this is

The Signal Ledger is the reference implementation of the Open Ring Protocol stack — ORP-01 (questions), ORP-02 (verdict), ORP-03 (Field record).

A ledger only means something when nobody owns it. Records here are signed Nostr events of kind 1337 — owned by the person who filed them, verifiable by anyone, stored across the relay network.

The framework holds itself to its own condition: α × P(detect) > 1. A signed Nostr event satisfies both. A Google Sheet satisfies neither.

The protocol stack
ORP-01 Question Protocol input layer The questions and answer types. ORP-02 Verdict Protocol processing layer The nine-field standard output. V.01 through V.09. ORP-03 Field Protocol output layer The signed record on Nostr kind 1337.
