What this is

The Signal Ledger is the reference implementation of the Open Ring Protocol v2 stack — ORP-01 (questions), ORP-02 (verdict), ORP-03 (Field record).

Records are signed Nostr events of kind 1337 — owned by the person who filed them, verifiable by anyone, stored across the relay network.

The framework holds itself to its own condition: α × P(detect) > 1. A signed Nostr event satisfies both. A Google Sheet satisfies neither.

How to connect

Uses NIP-07 — a browser extension that holds your Nostr private key and signs events without exposing the key.

Compatible: Alby, nos2x, Nostore (iOS), Kiwi Browser + nos2x (Android).
